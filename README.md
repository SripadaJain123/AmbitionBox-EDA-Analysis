# AmbitionBox EDA Analysis

## Project Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Top 500 companies listed on AmbitionBox to uncover hiring trends, employee satisfaction, workplace ratings, and industry insights across India.

---

## Objective
- Scrape company data from AmbitionBox
- Clean and preprocess the dataset
- Analyze hiring trends and employee reviews
- Visualize insights using charts and graphs
- Identify top industries and companies

---

## Key Insights

### Hiring Trends
- IT Services & Consulting dominates hiring across industries.
- Banking, Insurance, and Internet sectors also show strong hiring demand.

### Employee Satisfaction
- Job Security ratings are generally high.
- Work-Life Balance scores are moderate to high.
- Promotion and career growth satisfaction is comparatively lower.

### Top Job Locations
- Bangalore
- Mumbai
- Hyderabad
- Pune

### Top Companies
- Accenture leads in job openings.
- Marpu Foundation has one of the highest employee ratings.
